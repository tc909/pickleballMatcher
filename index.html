Social Double (社交優先模式)
Social Priority Mode
這是最受歡迎的休閒模式。系統會追蹤「搭檔矩陣 (Partner Matrix)」，優先讓從未搭檔過、或很久沒搭檔的人組成一隊。目標是確保在一場球聚中，每個人都能與所有成員輪流合作。
The primary goal is variety. The algorithm checks historical data to pair players who haven't played together recently, ensuring a highly social and dynamic experience for everyone.
Round Robin (標準循環賽)
Tournament Round Robin
這是正式比賽使用的模式。系統會根據標準循環賽公式（如旋轉法）生成對戰表。重點在於對手與搭檔的「數學公平性」，確保每個人的總場次完全一致，適合需要計算積分的競賽。
Focuses on mathematical fairness. Matches are generated using standard tournament rotation schemes (Carousel Method). Ideal for competitive play where game counts and point tracking are critical.
Max Consecutive (連場限制)
Stamina & Rotation Management
此功能用來強制保護體力。當設定為 Max 2 時，球員打完兩場後必須休息一場。在 5-6 人局中，為了維持配對流暢度，建議放寬至 Max 3。
A safety valve for player stamina. Max 2 forces a break after two consecutive games. For small groups (5-6 players), Max 3 is recommended to prevent logic deadlocks.
